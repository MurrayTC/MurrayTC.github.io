What are you making?

This is all I could make. It's all coded in HTML with a tiny amount of JavaScript, no PHP. I don't worship Satan. God, I still hate what that language did to me...
I'm making a game. So basically here's the game summarized: You're one of the god-knows-how-many people immune to the virus, in a world struck by an epidemic, and
along came zombies. The game is set around 2012, with the mechanics of survival and crafting, but also weight, in order for a more realistic approach. The game might
also possibly be loosely based off of Contagion, and also Train to Busan, No More Room in Hell, and a few Roblox games.
