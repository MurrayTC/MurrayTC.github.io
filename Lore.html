Mikatsu Co., Ltd

Mikatsu Co., Ltd (三且付株式会社 Mikatsu Kabushik Kaisha) is a multinational conglomerate founded as a result of two companies merging in 1976.
The name of the company was derived from MikaMedia (メディア), a publishing company founded 1962, and Tsukumogami (付九喪合三) Co., an architectural
firm founded 1954.

After their foundation, they started expanding to other fields, such as health care and energy. In 1995, they opened Mikatsu R&L, a research firm
dedicated for health care and other clandestine experiments, albeit the latter in a smaller quantity.
